### Thursday, April 16 – Normandy (Full Day)

Spend the full day exploring Normandy D-Day sites.

Plan to start with the westernmost sites and work your way east. Go at your own pace and spend more time at the places that matter most.

**Notes:**
- Pack lunches to save time
- Stay flexible — this is a big day with a lot to see

---

**Morning**

**Late Morning / Midday**

**Evening**

### Saturday, April 19 – Grandpa's WWII Sites

**Morning**
- Eat breakfast
- Pick up Albert Trosdorf at his house and tour brick factory and Frenzerburg.
  **Schlossstrasse 12, 52379 Langerwehe-Merode**

**Afternoon & Evening**
- Grab lunch on the road
- Visit meaningful sites connected to Grandpa:
  - Schevenhütte (where he first arrived on the front lines)
  - Höfen (where he was wounded)

**Evening Options**
- Drive back after dinner
- OR head back earlier if everyone is tired and have dinner closer to home base

  **23 Rue de Nantes, Mitry-Mory, Île-de-France 77290**
